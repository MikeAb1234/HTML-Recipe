Good Ole' Chicken


Ingredients

  • 6 chicken drumsticks
  • 6 chicken thighs
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Seasoned Salt
  • 3 cups Flour
  • 3 quarts Peanut Oil

Directions

  1. Clean the chicken pieces with warm water.
  2. Dry the chicken with paper towels.
  3. Season the chicken generously.
  4. Heat the oil on medium-high.
  5. Season the flour.
